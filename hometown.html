Hometown

UI/UX Design | Product Design


I created a concept design for a mobile app named Hometown which would be a hub for people to show off where they live. It could be a pizza place, the cool new club that just opened, or the best romantic spot. Users get the inside scoop of what is happening near them by checking their feed, which is a timeline of photos taken within their city or location.

The interface is full of intuitive swiping motions to implement filters, captions, and hashtags. For this concept, I wanted to really highlight the photos, therefore the interface is very stripped down and not too intruding. Simple, intuitive, yet powerful.
