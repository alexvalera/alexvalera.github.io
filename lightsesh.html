Web App Design/Developmentt


During the summer of 2014, I began a creating a time management and study tool as a personal project. I based this lightweight tool on a studying technique called the "Pomodoro Technique" created by Francesco Cirillo. Essentially, this tool allows you to easily track your time studying/working in 30 minute intervals. Users can also add "missions" to complete for their study or work session and also take notes that can be saved into the browser. This was also my introduction into the javascript framework, AngularJS.

For the design, I wanted to implement a calming interface which allowed users easily and effectively track their tasks and keep track of their time. I wanted to the interactions to be non-intrusive, simple, and provide quick feedback (for instance the save notes button). For the visual design, I looked at soft colors and went with a beautiful but subtle gradient background with lots of blue (which according to psychology, is good for boosting creativity).
